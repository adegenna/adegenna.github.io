Welcome! I'm Anthony, a computational scientist and engineer at Brookhaven National Laboratory. I specialize in uncertainty quantification, machine learning, and scientific computing. Before joining Brookhaven, I completed my Ph.D. in Mechanical/Aerospace Engineering at Princeton University under Clarence Rowley, and then did a postdoc at Los Alamos National Laboratory. My research centers around developing and applying these methods to physical systems and experimental data.

In my spare time, I dabble in art and writing, playing guitar, and recreational coding for math and physics-based programs.
