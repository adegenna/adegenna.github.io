Fiction and poetry

"The Pier"

I wrote this to explore the idea of belonging, and how self-contradictory it can be. I think we can find ourselves fully in a place, fully out of it, and somewhere in between, all at the same time.

"Gray Skies"

I've always loved the idea that aesthetics is as much a product of the observer as it is of the creator. I try to capture that in this poem.
